Most fitness apps either drown you in data or treat you like a beginner.

Macro Monk is different: practical, fast, and built around one question every morning:

**What should I do today to keep progressing without burning out?**

## What makes Macro Monk different

### Morning decision engine

The app gives a daily training recommendation using recovery, yesterday's load, and trend signals.

### Recovery-first flow

No fake "go hard" hype before check-in. Log recovery first, then get a recommendation you can trust.

### Orthostatic test support (optional)

For serious lifters and coaches: supine + standing HR tracking with clear green/yellow/red guidance. If orthostatic is red two days in a row, the app pushes recovery or rest.

### Phase check-in with coach logic

Cut, bulk, and maintenance tracking that turns trends into actionable guidance.

### Macros without overcomplication

Set daily targets manually, or use the phase calculator and apply results in one click.

### Fast food logging that actually works

Favorites, default food database, personal foods, and quick filters built for real daily use.

### Clean UX

Lightweight, fast, no noise. Just the signals and tools that matter.
